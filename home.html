Life on Land
"Our mission is to create a profound awareness of the interdependence of all land life by empowering people and communities to take care of terrestrial ecosystems. We promote conservation, restoration, and sustainable management techniques that safeguard biodiversity, protect endangered species, and preserve the essential resources that all life depends on via advocacy, education, and creative solutions. Together, we predict a world where humankind and the natural world live peacefully, protecting the planet's health and vitality for present and future generations."
